# Saturday Shakes — Logo Options

Brand stage: obsidian `#0B0C10`. Accent: copper/gold `#B87333`, support `#D4975C`.
Family grammar: geometric S skeleton, optical precision (sibling of the reduced-S parent
and the game-tile Solutions mark). Across all options the **S reads first, the shaker second**.

## Option A — Contour Shaker  (`shakes-a.svg`, `shakes-a-mono.svg`)
- **Concept:** Faithful vectorisation of the owner's preferred reference — a double-line
  contour drawing of a 2-tin Boston shaker whose two offset tins form an "S": top tin =
  upper bowl (mouth up-right), bottom tin = lower bowl (mouth down-left), the spine emerging
  from the diagonal overlap seam.
- **Rationale:** Every "line" is an authored FILLED band (~12u closed ring, never a stroke),
  so the parallel double-contour rhythm and gentle entasis survive scaling; the two contour
  lines stay ≥10u apart for legibility down to 32px.
- **Accent:** Solid copper `#B87333` throughout; mono variant is pure `currentColor`.

## Option B — Solid Tin S  (`shakes-b.svg`, `shakes-b-mono.svg`)
- **Concept:** The same two tins as bold solid silhouettes. The overlap seam between them is
  carved as negative space so the S-curve reads as a bright diagonal break.
- **Rationale:** High-impact, small-size-proof mark for app icons and favicons where thin
  contours would fill in. Two `#D4975C` facet slivers down the lit flanks give brushed-metal
  dimension without a gradient.
- **Accent:** Copper `#B87333` bodies, `#D4975C` facets, seam knocked to obsidian `#0B0C10`.
  Mono cuts the seam as a true transparent gap (evenodd) and drops the second tone.

## Option C — Minimal Pour  (`shakes-c.svg`, `shakes-c-mono.svg`)
- **Concept:** Maximally abstracted — two tapered capsule tin-profiles (with entasis) tilted
  and offset so together they read as an S, a neutral monoline pour-gap tracing the spine.
- **Rationale:** The reductive, modern end of the range; strongest at tiny sizes and as a
  motion/animation cue (the gap is the "pour" line between the two tins). Duotone keeps it
  crisp and confident.
- **Accent:** Copper `#B87333` capsules, neutral `#F0F3F4` monoline gap. Mono renders both
  forms in `currentColor` with the pour-gap cut as a transparent evenodd slot.
