# Saturday Services — Parent Mark Options

The parent must be the *most reduced* S of the family: a geometric S skeleton with
45° chamfer details, optical precision, and equal presence at favicon scale and as a
3D-extruded hero sculpture. All three share one underlying S-spine (two half-circle
bowls, upper counter tightened for optical top/bottom balance) so the family reads
as one hand.

## Option A — "Meridian S"
- **Concept:** A reduced monoline S built from two interlocking semicircular arcs
  (weight 64u), each terminal cut with a flat 45° chamfer. The most timeless and
  scalable of the three — the natural default parent mark and the cleanest to extrude.
- **Rationale:** Pure geometry, no ornament. Chrome upper bowl over titanium-white
  lower bowl gives a subtle hard-surface material shift without breaking the monoline.
- **Accent:** Electric blue sits *only* on the central seam — the vertical segment
  where the two arcs pass one another — carrying the single permitted blue gradient.

## Option B — "Plate S"
- **Concept:** A solid chamfered-octagon plate in titanium (#1F2833) with the S carved
  as negative space (fill-rule evenodd) and a thin chrome edge ring tracing the bevel.
  Reads as a machined badge — strong as an app icon or embossed hardware mark.
- **Rationale:** Mass and edge, not line. The octagon supplies the "hard-surface,
  metallic, restrained" body; the carved S keeps it unmistakably the family letter.
- **Accent:** One small electric-blue chamfer notch on the top-right bevel — a signal,
  never wallpaper. Mono version inverts to a solid-S inked stamp inside the frame ring.

## Option C — "Ribbon S"
- **Concept:** One continuous folded ribbon traced through the S in 13 flat facets;
  alternating fold faces use two flat tones (high-def white #F0F3F4 / deep desaturated
  blue #0891A1) to fake dimension with flat fills only — no gradient, shadow, or filter.
- **Rationale:** Energy with restraint — the fold facets give motion and depth while the
  silhouette stays a disciplined geometric S. The most expressive of the three.
- **Accent:** The desaturated electric-blue tone *is* the accent, alternating across the
  fold faces. Mono version merges to one silhouette with thin counter-gap slits at each
  fold so the folded read survives at a single ink color.
